# Prime Renovation Landing Page – Hero/Header Section Design

**Date:** 2026-04-18

## Overview
The Hero/Header section is the first and most prominent part of the Prime Renovation landing page. It is designed to immediately establish a premium, trustworthy brand impression and drive users to request a free estimate.

## Visual & UI Principles
- Uses shadcn/ui components for modern, accessible, and customizable UI elements.
- Applies your preferred color pattern for a premium, clean, and elegant look.
- Responsive and mobile-friendly, with a visually strong layout.

## Layout
- **Full-width hero area** at the top of the page.
- **Left:** Automated image showcase (carousel/slider) with high-quality renovation photos (bathroom, kitchen, house).
- **Right:**
  - Short business introduction (1–2 sentences).
  - Prominent free quotation form:
    - Name (text)
    - Email (email)
    - Phone (tel)
    - Service Type (dropdown: Bathroom, Kitchen, House)
    - Project Details (textarea)
    - Submit button (primary, labeled “Get Free Estimate”)
  - Clear call to action above or within the form.
- **Mobile:** Stacks vertically, with the form below the image showcase.

## Visual Style
- Clean, premium, and modern.
- Elegant typography (serif or high-end sans-serif for headings).
- Subtle gradients or overlays for text readability over images.
- Button and form styled for high contrast and accessibility.

## Functionality
- Image showcase auto-advances every 3–5 seconds, with manual navigation (arrows/dots).
- Form validates input and provides clear feedback.
- On submit, shows a success message or error.

## shadcn/ui Usage
- Use shadcn/ui components for the form, buttons, and layout primitives.
- Customize components to match your color palette and branding.

## Color Pattern
- Apply your preferred color pattern throughout the section for consistency and brand alignment.
